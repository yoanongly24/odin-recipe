Udang Saos ala Ijum

Hey ma look what I made!

Like shrimp but tired of eating just boiled shrimp? Follow along this easy recipe

Ingredients

  • Half an onion
  • 4 cloves Scallions
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • 500 grams Shrimp
  • Sauce: 2 tbsp Tomato Sauce, 1 tbsp Oyster Sauce, 1 tbsp Sugar

Cooking Instructions

  1. Finely chop scallions and garlic
  2. Chop the onion into thin strands
  3. Mix sauce evenly
  4. Brown the onion, scallion and garlic with a frying pan in this order
  5. Throw in the shrimp until it turns red
  6. Add the sauce and an ice-crean tester spoon worth of sal. Stir for a bit
  7. Add a shotglass of water and stir for a bit of saucy consistency
